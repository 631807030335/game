Here is a brief introduction

This column includes League of Legends

type

League of Heroes (LOL) is a MoBa competitive online game developed by American fist games and operated by Tencent in mainland China. There are hundreds of personality heroes in the game, and it has the ranking system, rune system and other characteristic cultivation system..

Development achievements

League of heroes is committed to promoting the development of global E-sports. In addition to developing professional leagues and building E-sports system, League of heroes will h old three world-class competitions, namely "League of heroes mid season championship", "League of heroes global finals" and "League of heroes all star" every year, forming its own unique E-sports culture.

LPL honor

On November 3, 2018, Ig won the championship in the global finals of the 2018 League of heroes, which is the first s Championship for LPL teams in history; On November 10, 2019, FPX won the championship in the global finals of the 2019 League of heroes, which is the second s Championship for LPL teams in history! On May 31, 2020, the two LPL teams FPX and tes successfully joined in the finals of the 2020 League of heroes mid season cup. This is the first time in history that LPL teams joined in the finals of international competitions!

The team that won the s competition over the years

S2 global finals

Venue: USA

The second league of heroes global finals was held in the United States, with 12 teams, including 2 teams from China and South Korea, 3 teams from North America and Europe, and 2 teams from Southeast Asia GPL Super League. The finals of the second league of heroes global finals opened in Los Angeles on the morning of October 14, 2012 Beijing time. The champion was won by Taiwan's assassin star

S3 global finals

Venue：Southern California

The third league of heroes global finals was held in the United States, with 14 teams, 14 top teams from North America, South Korea, China, Southeast Asia, Europe and one wild card will gather in Southern California,Fight for the championship The finals of the third league of heroes global finals opened in Los Angeles on the morning of 2013 Beijing time. The champion was won by SKT in Korea

S4 global finals

Venue：Korea, Singapore, Taiwan

The 2014 global finals of League of heroes is the fourth season of lol. From the competition of 16 top teams in the world The opening match will be held on September 18. The group matches will be held in Taipei, China and Singapore respectively. The knockout matches will be held in Busan and Seoul, South Korea. The champion was won by South Korea's Samsung.

S5 global finals

Venue：Britain， France， Germany and Belgium

The third league of heroes global finals was held in the United States, with 16 teams, 16 top teams from North America, South Korea, China, Southeast Asia, Europe and one wild card will gather Britain France Germany Belgium,Fight for the championship The finals of the third league of heroes global finals opened in Los Angeles on the morning of 2013 Beijing time. The champion was won by SKT in Korea.

S6 global finals

Venue：America

The 2016 League of heroes global finals is a five week international E-sports event with 16 teams participating in league of heroes. The 2016 global finals will start from the group stage in San Francisco, then move to Chicago for the quarter finals, and then to New York City for the semi-finals. In the end, the final will return to the Staples Center in Los Angeles on the west coast, and SKT of South Korea will win the championship

S7 global finals

Venue：china

The 2017 League of heroes global finals is a five week international E-sports event with League of heroes as its project The 2017 global finals start from the shortlisted and group stage in Wuhan, then move to Guangzhou for the quarter finals, and then go to Shanghai for the semi-finals. Finally, the final was held at the National Stadium "bird's nest" in Beijing. Champion: South Korea's SSG team

S8 global finals

Venue：Korea

The 2018 League of heroes global finals is an international E-sports event hosted by riot games in South Korea. There are 24 teams in four cities and venues in South Korea to attack the summoner cup. On November 3, 2018, Ig, the LPL division team, defeated FNC team from European LCS division 3-0 and won the championship of the global finals, which is the first championship won by LPL division in the history of the global finals.

S9 global finals

The final of the 2019 League of heroes Global final is held at the Accor hotel stadium in Paris, the city of light. On November 10, 2019, FPX team won the championship by defeating G2 team 3-0.
